About Our Employee Management App

Welcome to our Employee Management App, designed to streamline your workforce administration effortlessly. Here's a quick overview of its key functionalities:

  1. Show Employees: View a tabular list of all employees with options to edit or delete. Clicking on "Edit" allows you to modify employee details, with a "Save" button to confirm changes. For direct deletion, use the dedicated "Delete Employees" button.
  2. Add Employees: Access a user-friendly form where you can enter new employee information. Submit the form to add a new member to your workforce database.
  3. Delete Employees: Efficiently remove any employee from the database by selecting their email ID from a list of current employees.

Manage your team effortlessly with our intuitive Employee Management App. Simplify your operations and stay organized with just a few clicks.
